# Getting started

Admins manage Users, cases and assignments related tasks. 
They are available to select user to view. also available to select case to change.

![support1](img/support1.png)

## Attendance

Click **Attendance**, Type Tutor's name and select the name and clinic, enter date, session and student group then Click **Next**.

![support2](img/support2.png)

Select students and click **Save**.
![support3](img/support3.png)


 Information of the students will be shown on the right hand side of the page, listed **Attendance taken for today (number of students)** 

![support4](img/support4.png)

## Admin
Once you enter the Login page, the **Admin** section will be shown on the page, admin staff can add assignments, edit and view cases and user by clicking **Admin** or **View admin page**. You will enter the **Site administration** by clicking **Admin** or **View admin page**.

![support5](img/support5.png)
- Assessment
1. Click **Add** to add the assignment, enter name, description, select group and fill in the submission due date, grade due date and the confirmed due date.

![support6](img/support6.png)

2. "The assignment 'name; was added successfully" will be shown when you successfully added assignment.

![support7](img/support7.png)

3. Admin can also generate report when the assignment grade is out. Select the assignment and choose **Generate report**, click **Run**.

![support8](img/support8.png)


- Case
Click **Cases** or **Change** can view and edit cases.
![support9](img/support9.png)
You can change details or status of the case by clicking the title of the case list
![support10](img/support10.png)

Click **SAVE** or **Save and continue editing** to save changes.

![support11](img/support11.png)

There are three actions for admin to select - Lock cases, share cases and assign the case to teachers or tutor for assessment. Select the case (check the box) and Click **Run** to continue.

![support12](img/support12.png)
